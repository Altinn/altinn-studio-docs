Authorization

Learn how Dialogporten uses Altinn Authorization and provides its own authorization mechanisms

Introduction

Dialogporten is fully integrated with ID-porten, Maskinporten and Altinn Authorization. Access to all dialogs are subject to the same access policy as the service they represent, which is fully manageable via roles, access packages and service rights in Altinn Access Management.
