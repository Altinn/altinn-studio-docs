## Introduction

All dialogs in Dialogporten must refer to a "resource", which is the entity that defines the "kind" of dialog, e.g., message or form service, the service owner, and authorization policies, among other things.

## Basic steps

Resources are administered via Altinn Studio Designer or via API.

Please refer to the guide {{<link "../../../../authorization/guides/resource-owner/create-resource-resource-admin/" title>}} for more information.
